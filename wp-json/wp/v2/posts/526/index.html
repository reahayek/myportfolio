Khatt Books<\/b>\u00a0is a publishing house that\u00a0focuses on topics such as typography, graphic design, architecture, other applied and visual arts from or dealing with the MENA region.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n

\n

During the summer of 2018, I worked as a Graphic Design intern on three volumes of Khatt Books, under the art direction of Huda Smitshuijzen AbiFar\u00e8s, the founding director of\u00a0<\/span>Khatt Foundation<\/a>. I initiated and developed the concept and layout design of the books based on the work of the three respective Arab artists.<\/p>\n<\/div>\n
