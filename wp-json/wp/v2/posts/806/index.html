Every semester, the department of Architecture and Design (ArD) at the American University of Beirut (AUB) hosts a number of architects, designers and artists to give talks and lectures at the faculty. While completing my BFA in Graphic Design and working as assistant to Professor Reza Abedini, I was responsible for creating the visuals for posters for the ArD Lecture Series<\/em><\/strong>, for almost 2 years.<\/p>

The department also held other bigger scale events, talks, award ceremonies etc. for which I designed posters, invitations banners and other promotional material.\u00a0Here are a few.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
