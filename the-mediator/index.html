the mediator

Motions

A title sequence of 47 seconds for a television series based on Meg Cabot’s fictional book The Mediator. The animation is, in parts, done in stop-motion. The diary reflects the main character – a teenager who leads a double life: average high-school girl by day, fighting the dead by night.
